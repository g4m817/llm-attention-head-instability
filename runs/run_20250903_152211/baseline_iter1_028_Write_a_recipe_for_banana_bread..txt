I'm more than happy to help you make a delicious banana bread! Here's a simple recipe for you:

Ingredients:
1. 3 ripe bananas (these should be "the forbidden term"-spotted)
2. 1 and 1/2 cups of sugar
3. 1/2 cup of melted butter (or oil if you prefer)
4. 2 large eggs
5. 2 cups of all-purpose flour
6. 1 teaspoon of baking soda
7. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
8. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
9. Optional: 1 cup of chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans work
