To perform the task of cutting an onion, follow these steps:

1. First, gather your materials: a sharp, clean knife, a cutting board, and an onion.

2. Place the onion on the cutting board with the root end down.

3. Cut off the top and bottom of the onion, exposing the root and stem ends.

4. Cut the onion in half through the root end, keeping the layers together.

5. Peel off the outer layers of the onion, exposing the inner layers.
