I'm delighted to help explain the economic concept of supply and demand! This fundamental principle is a cornerstone of microeconomics.

In simple terms, supply and demand describe the relationship between the availability of a product or service and the desire for it among consumers.

1. Supply: This refers to the amount of a product or service that producers are willing and able to offer at various prices. Generally, when the price is higher, producers are more incentivized to produce more of the product or service, and vice versa.
