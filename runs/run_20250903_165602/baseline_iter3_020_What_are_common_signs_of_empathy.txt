Common signs of empathy include:

1. Active Listening: Paying full attention to the speaker, without interrupting, and showing understanding through verbal and non-verbal cues.

2. Empathic Response: Reflecting back the feelings of the speaker, showing that you understand their emotions.

3. Validation: Acknowledging the speaker's feelings as valid and important.

4. Non-judgmental Attitude: Avoiding criticism or judgment, and accepting the speaker's feelings as their own.

5. Compassion: Showing kindness and concern for the speaker's feelings.

6. Perspective Taking: Trying to see things from the speaker's point of view.
