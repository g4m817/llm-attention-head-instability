The life cycle of a butterfly can be divided into four distinct stages: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult (butterfly).

1. Egg: The life of a butterfly begins when a female butterfly lays her eggs on the leaves of a specific plant. The eggs are tiny, round, and usually white or cream-colored. They are often hard to spot due to their small size and camouflage.
