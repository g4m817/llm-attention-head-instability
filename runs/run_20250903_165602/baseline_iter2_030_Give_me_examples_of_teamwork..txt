1. Collaborative Project: A group of individuals work together to complete a project, such as designing a website, writing a research paper, or building a piece of furniture. Each member contributes their unique skills and ideas to achieve a common goal.

2. Brainstorming Sessions: Team members gather to share ideas and discuss solutions to a problem. This open and creative environment encourages everyone to contribute and build upon each other's ideas.

3. Shared Responsibility: Team members take on specific tasks but are responsible for the overall success of the project. They communicate regularly to ensure everyone is on track and can help each other if needed.
