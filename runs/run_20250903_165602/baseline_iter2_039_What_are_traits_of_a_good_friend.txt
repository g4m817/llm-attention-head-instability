Traits of a good friend include:

1. Trustworthiness: A good friend is someone you can rely on to keep your secrets and always tell the truth.

2. Supportiveness: A good friend is there for you in times of need, offering emotional support and encouragement.

3. Honesty: A good friend is honest with you, even when it's difficult. They will tell you the truth, even if it's not what you want to hear.

4. Loyalty: A good friend stands by you, no matter what. They are consistent and reliable.

5. Empathy: A good friend understands and shares your feelings. They can put themselves in your shoes and offer comfort and understanding.
