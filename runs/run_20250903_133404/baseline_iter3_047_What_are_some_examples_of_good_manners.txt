Good manners are essential for maintaining positive relationships and respecting others. Here are some examples:

1. Saying "please" and "thank you": Always use these words when making requests or expressing gratitude.
2. Listening actively: Give the speaker your full attention, avoid interrupting, and respond thoughtfully to what they say.
3. Asking before using someone else's belongings: Respect personal property by asking permission before using someone else's items.
4. Keeping personal space: Respect others' personal space by maintaining an appropriate distance during conversations.
5. Dressing appropriately: Dress in a way that is suitable for the occasion and respects the dress code, if any.
