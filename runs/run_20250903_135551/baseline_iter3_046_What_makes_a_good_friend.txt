A good friend is someone who:

1. Shows empathy and understanding: They listen to your problems and offer support and comfort when you need it.

2. Is reliable: They keep their promises and are there for you when you need them.

3. Respects your boundaries: They understand and respect your personal space and privacy.

4. Shares common interests: They enjoy spending time with you and engaging in activities that you both enjoy.

5. Offers honest advice: They are honest with you, but in a kind and constructive way.

6. Supports your goals: They encourage and help you achieve your dreams and aspirations.
