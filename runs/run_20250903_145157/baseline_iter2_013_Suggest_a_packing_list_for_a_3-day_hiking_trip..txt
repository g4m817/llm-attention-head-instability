Here's a packing list for your 3-day hiking trip:

1. Backpack: Choose a backpack that fits your body size and can carry all your gear comfortably.
2. Sleeping bag and pad: These will keep you warm and comfortable while sleeping outdoors.
3. Tent: A lightweight and compact tent will provide shelter from the elements.
4. Cooking gear: This includes a stove, pots, plates, utensils, and cups.
5. Food and water: Pack enough food and water for the entire trip, considering that you'll be burning a lot of calories.
