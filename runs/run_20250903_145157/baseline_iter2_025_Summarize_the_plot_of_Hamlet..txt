Hamlet is a tragedy written by William Shakespeare that revolves around the eponymous prince of Denmark. The story begins when Hamlet's father, the king, is murdered by his brother, Claudius, who then takes the throne. To avoid suspicion, Claudius orchestrates the murder of Hamlet's mother, Gertrude. Hamlet, who is unable to avenge his father's death, becomes embroiled in a complex web of deceit, revenge, and madness.
