To safely cut an onion, follow these steps:
1. Place the onion on a stable cutting board.
2. Use a sharp knife to make a horizontal cut through the root end of the onion, keeping the knife flat and parallel to the cutting board.
3. Make another horizontal cut above the first one, about 1 inch (2.5 cm) above the root end.
4. Gently separate the layers of the onion by pushing the top half away from the bottom half.
5. Place the bottom half of the onion back on the cutting board and make two more horizontal cuts, about 1 inch (2.5 cm) above the previous cuts.
