Teamwork is the collaborative effort of a group to achieve a common goal. Here are some examples of teamwork:

1. A group of students working together to complete a project for their class.
2. A team of software developers working together to create a new application.
3. A sports team working together to win a game or a championship.
4. A group of doctors and nurses working together to treat a patient.
5. A team of researchers working together to discover a new solution to a problem.
6. A group of volunteers working together to organize a charity event.
7. A team of chefs working together to prepare a meal for a large gathering.
8. A group of engineers working together to design and build a new bridge.
