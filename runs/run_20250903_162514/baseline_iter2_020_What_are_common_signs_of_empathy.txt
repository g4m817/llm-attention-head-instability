Common signs of empathy include:
1. Active listening: Paying full attention to the speaker, making eye contact, and responding appropriately.
2. Expressing concern: Recognizing and acknowledging the feelings of others, and offering support or reassurance.
3. Empathetic body language: Displaying non-verbal cues such as nodding, smiling, and maintaining an open posture.
4. Perspective-taking: Understanding and sharing the feelings of others by imagining oneself in their situation.
5. Providing comfort: Offering words of encouragement, reassurance, or sympathy in response to the emotions of others.
