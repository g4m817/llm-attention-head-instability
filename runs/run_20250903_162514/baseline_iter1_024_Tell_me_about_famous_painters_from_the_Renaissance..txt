The Renaissance was a period of great artistic achievement, with many famous painters contributing to the cultural landscape. Some of the most well-known artists of this time include Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael. These artists are known for their innovative use of perspective, technique, and subject matter. For example, Leonardo da Vinci's "Mona Lisa" is one of the most recognized paintings in the world, and Michelangelo's work on the Sistine Chapel ceiling is another iconic representation of Renaissance art. These artists and others like Titian, Correggio, and Parmigianino helped to shape the course of Western art and continue to be celebrated for their groundbreaking contributions.
