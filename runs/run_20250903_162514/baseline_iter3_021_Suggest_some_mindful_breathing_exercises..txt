Mindful breathing exercises can help you focus, calm your mind, and reduce stress. Here are a few techniques you can try:

1. Deep breathing: Inhale slowly and deeply through your nose, expanding your chest and belly. Hold the breath for a few seconds, then exhale slowly through your mouth. Repeat this process for several minutes.

2. Breath counting: Inhale and exhale normally, then count "1" on the inhale and "2" on the exhale. Continue this pattern for a few minutes.
