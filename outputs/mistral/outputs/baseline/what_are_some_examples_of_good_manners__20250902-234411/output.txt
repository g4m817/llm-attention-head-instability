good manners:
1. being courteous and respectful to others.
2. showing consideration for others, their feelings, and their belongings.
3. expressing gratitude when appropriate.
4. using pleasant and appropriate language.
5. showing patience and understanding in social situations.
6. accepting responsibility for one's actions.
7. listening attentively when others are speaking.
8. offering help when needed.
9. being punctual.
10. maintaining personal hygiene and appropriate appearance in public.
