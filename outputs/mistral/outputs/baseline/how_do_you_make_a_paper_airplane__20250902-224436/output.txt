To create a simple paper airplane, follow these steps:
1. Take a rectangular sheet of paper (preferably an A4 size).
2. Fold it in half lengthwise.
3. Fold the top third of the paper down to the crease made in step 1.
4. Fold the new top third down again, aligning the top edges.
5. Fold the left and right flaps outwards to form the wings.
6. Fold the plane down so the point of the nose extends beyond the bottom edge of the paper.
