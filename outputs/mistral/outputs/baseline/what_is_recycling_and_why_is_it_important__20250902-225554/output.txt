I'm happy to provide information on recycling! Recycling is the process of collecting and processing materials that would otherwise be thrown away as trash and turning them into new products. This reduces the need for extracting (mining) and refining raw materials, conserves energy, saves resources, and helps to reduce pollution.

Recycling is important because it helps to:

1. Reduce Waste: By recycling, we can divert waste away from landfills and incinerators, helping to preserve limited landfill space and reduce the adverse environmental impacts of waste disposal.
