The life cycle of a butterfly can be described as follows:
1. Egg: The life of a butterfly begins with an egg, which is laid by the female butterfly on a leaf or stem.
2. Larva (Caterpillar): After approximately 1 week, the egg hatches into a caterpillar. The caterpillar eats and grows for several weeks, shedding its skin four times during this stage.
