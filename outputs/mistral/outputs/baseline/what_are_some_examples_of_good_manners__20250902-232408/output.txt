1. Table manners - covering your mouth when yawning, not talking with your mouth full, using utensils correctly, and asking to be excused before leaving the table are all important table manners.

2. Social media interaction - refraining from posting offensive or hurtful comments, responding politely to others' content, and using appropriate language are good practices to follow when using social media.
