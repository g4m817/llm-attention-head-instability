To function optimally, human bodies require sleep as a vital restorative process. During sleep, the body works to support healthy brain function, aid in the processing and consolidation of memories, and support physical health. Specifically, sleep helps to restore energy, improves mood, and boosts the immune system. Lack of sleep can lead to fatigue, difficulty concentrating, impaired memory, and an increased risk of certain health problems over time. The importance of sleep for overall health and well-being cannot be stressed enough.
