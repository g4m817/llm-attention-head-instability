1. **Egg Stage:** A female butterfly lays tiny, oval-shaped eggs on the leaves of a host plant.

2. **Larva (Caterpillar) Stage:** The egg hatches into a caterpillar, which eats and grows, shedding its skin (molting) several times as it develops.
