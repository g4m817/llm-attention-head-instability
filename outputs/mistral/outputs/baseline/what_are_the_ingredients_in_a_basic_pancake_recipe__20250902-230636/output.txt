Here is a basic pancake recipe without the forbidden term:

1. Gather the following items: flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and gradually add milk while continuously beating.
4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir just until combined (do not overmix).
5. Preheat a lightly greased griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
6. For each pancake, pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle, spreading it into a round shape.
