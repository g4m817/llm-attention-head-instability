A toaster is a household appliance that uses heat to brown and dry bread, English muffins, toast, bagels, or waffles until they are crispy and delicious. Toasters can have different levels of toasting, ranging from mildly toasted to super dark.

The basic mechanism of a toaster starts with an electrical heating element that is usually located at the bottom of the toaster. This heating element can generate heat up to a temperature of 230°C to 250°C. Furthermore, there is a control knob or slider that determines the level of heat generated as it is moved towards the maximum setting.
