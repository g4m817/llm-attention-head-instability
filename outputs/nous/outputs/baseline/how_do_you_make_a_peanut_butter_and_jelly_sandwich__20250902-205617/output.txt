To make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich, you will need the following ingredients and utensils:

Ingredients:
1. Bread (your choice of type and number of slices)
2. Peanut butter (preferably smooth)
3. Jelly or jam (your choice of flavor)

Utensils:
1. Spoon for measuring and spreading peanut butter
2. Knife for spreading jelly and assembling the sandwich
3. Plate or cutting board for working on

Before you start:
Make sure to gather and measure all your ingredients and tools for easy access while making the sandwich.
