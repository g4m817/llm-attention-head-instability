A cat is a small domesticated mammal with soft fur and four legs, usually round ears, and a tail of varying lengths. It has a muscular body, and it is typically covered with hairs of different lengths and textures. Cats are generally known for being independent, smart, and playful. They can have various colors and patterns on their fur, such as brown, black, gray, orange, or white. Cat owners often characterize their pets as loving and affectionate, with a knack for landing on their feet.
